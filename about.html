What we are

We are a dynamic team of innovative thinkers.

Gadgid Tech is a UK-based reseller specializing in high-quality computer hardware and building refurbishment supplies. Serving both businesses and individuals, we deliver reliable technology and building solutions tailored to meet diverse needs.

Whether it's advanced computer parts for companies or essential building equipment for individuals, schools, and businesses, we are committed to quality and exceptional service. Our portfolio includes trusted subsidiary companies such as GJ Taylor & Company Ltd and PCBox Expert Center, broadening our reach and expertise in both the tech and building industries. At Gadgid Tech, we empower projects of all sizes with products that stand the test of time.

01.Our Mission

At Gadgid Tech, our mission is to deliver high-quality computer hardware and building supplies that enhance productivity and foster creativity. We are committed to providing exceptional customer service that meet the unique needs of our clients

02.Vission

To be the leading provider of innovative technology and building solutions across Europe, empowering businesses and individuals to achieve their goals with quality products and exceptional service.

03.Our Approach

At Gadgid Tech, we focus on understanding our clients' needs through a customer-centric approach. By delivering quality products, expert support, and innovative solutions, we empower businesses and individuals to succeed while fostering lasting partnerships across Europe.
